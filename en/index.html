About

Brisa is a Portuguese singer and songwriter known for writing relatable lyrics that come alive in contagious melodies.

She stands out for her ability to convey messages with genuineness and a desire to break the routine, bringing a breath of fresh air mixed with an adrenaline shot to your daily life.

In her debut EP “CASULO”, the singer takes us on a journey through the cycle of emotions, from the anxiety of "Nuvem" to the final transformation in "Metamorfose".
