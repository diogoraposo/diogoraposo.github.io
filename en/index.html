About Brisa

Brisa is a portuguese singer and songwriter known for relatable lyrics that come alive in contagious melodies. The genuine way of spreading her message and the will to break the routine make Brisa sound like a breath of fresh hair, mixed with an adrenaline injection in your day-to-day life. After exploring different sounds, the singer takes us on a trip through the cycle of emotions in her debut EP “CASULO”. The journey begins with “Nuvem”, the first chapter that has been conquering hearts since 2021 and that introduces the topic of anxiety, overthinking and not being able to be in the present moment. The next one is “Férias de Mim” - written by Brisa, Francesco Meoli and Miguel Coimbra (D.A.M.A) and produced by Meoli - that reveals and goes deep into the cause of all this chaos, “a voz que não se cala em mim” (in english: “the voice that never shuts up inside of my head”): our subconscious. There comes the time to be aware of the emotional block and make the decision to go to “Outro Mar” - the third song, released in 2022 - written by Brisa and produced by Meoli. “Contra O Vento” - the fourth song - talks about the moment when one decides to release the need to control, to relax and trust the process. All of these ideas come together in the last song “Metamorfose” - written by Brisa, Gonzalo Tau and ARIEL and produced by ARIEL - that illustrates this cycle using the natural phenomenon as a metaphor.
