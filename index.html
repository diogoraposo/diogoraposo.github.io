About Brisa

Brisa is a portuguese singer and songwriter wishing to bring a feeling of lightness and a pleasant and sunny rest. It’s truly the dream that characterizes her path, standing out by the capability of spreading her message genuinely and with the will to break the routine, bringing a fresh breeze mixed with an adrenaline boost to your day to day life. In highlights, “Loop” is part of the Antena 3’s radio playlist and the most recent single “Nuvem” got her a spot on the Spotify playlists Pop PT and EQUAL Portugal. Always available to talk and very sociable, nothing brightens her day like a message from her listeners.
